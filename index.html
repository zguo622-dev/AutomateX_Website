The solution: Scribtive™ automates the generation and assurance of PACE documentation, anchoring every section to cited standards and past precedents.

AI-Powered Station Surveys

Train Operating Company · 55 Stations

The challenge: Survey 55 stations for hidden critical elements. Traditional inspections missed structural defects invisible at surface level.

The solution: Drones captured 360° imaging data. AI processed the survey information to identify defects, generate insights, and produce condition reports automatically.

The result: Hidden defects identified across the portfolio, safety risks addressed, and reporting time reduced dramatically.

Tender Automation for Strategic Framework

Southern Renew Enterprise (SRE)

The challenge: A small consultancy with no track record competing against established firms for one of 16 tactical design partner seats on a £35m, 10-year framework spanning CP7 and CP8.

The solution: Built an AI knowledge base that synthesised the business’s core capabilities against tender requirements. AI identified the strongest case studies, generated optimised responses for each section, and allowed the founder to focus purely on judgement and strategy.
